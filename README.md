## 🌟 Overview

**ConvoFluent** transforms language learning into an immersive, personalized journey.  
Instead of generic lessons, you learn through **AI-generated conversations, scenarios, and lessons** based on what *you actually care about* — whether that’s:

🎌 Anime  
✈️ Travel  
🍜 Food  
🎮 Gaming  
💬 Or literally any interest you choose

The platform dynamically adapts to your **preferences + progress**, making learning feel less like studying and more like living the language.

---

## 🧠 Core Idea

> People learn faster when they care about what they’re learning.

ConvoFluent’s AI continuously:
- Tracks your learning patterns
- Understands your interests
- Adjusts difficulty
- Updates lesson content dynamically

So every session feels **fresh, relevant, and motivating.**

---

## 🚀 Features

✨ **Interest-Driven Conversations**  
Practice real dialogues about topics you love.

🧩 **Adaptive Difficulty Engine**  
Lessons scale with your skill level automatically.

🎭 **Real-Life Scenarios**  
Simulate travel, meetings, ordering food, gaming chats, and more.

📊 **Progress Intelligence**  
AI analyzes mistakes and strengthens weak areas.

🎯 **Personalized Learning Path**  
No two learners experience the same curriculum.
